Azure Content Understanding in Foundry Tools is a Microsoft Foundry service available in the Azure portal that uses generative AI to process and ingest various content types into user-defined output formats. It streamlines the extraction, classification, and structuring of unstructured data, enhancing accuracy with multiple AI models and confidence scores. The service supports multiple use cases including intelligent document processing, agentic applications, search and retrieval-augmented generation, robotic process automation, analytics, and industry-specific applications such as tax automation, mortgage processing, and media asset management. Key components include inputs, analyzers, content extraction, segmentation, field extraction, confidence scores, grounding, contextualization, Foundry models, and structured output. Content Understanding is accessible via the Foundry Azure Resource and complemented by Content Understanding Studio and upcoming Foundry NextGen portal features.
