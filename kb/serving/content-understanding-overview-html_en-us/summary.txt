Azure Content Understanding in Foundry Tools is a Microsoft Foundry service available in the Azure portal that uses generative AI to process and ingest various content types into structured outputs. It accelerates time-to-value by enabling streamlined workflows, accurate field extraction, confidence scoring, and grounding, supporting multiple industry-specific applications such as tax automation, mortgage processing, invoice verification, and media asset management. The service includes key components like inputs, analyzers, content extraction, segmentation, field extraction, confidence scores, grounding, contextualization, Foundry models, and structured output. It supports intelligent document processing, agentic applications, RAG search, RPA, analytics, and classification workflows. Content Understanding Studio and the Foundry NextGen portal provide enhanced user experiences for building and improving analyzers. Quickstart guides are available to help users begin using the service.
